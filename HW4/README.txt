The format of the social_data.txt file is columns of data that are tab delimited.
The end of each entry (town) is indicated with a (Unix) carrige return. Going
from left to right, the data in the columns is:

0-2 Name of Town and ZIP code.
3 Latitude
4 Longitude
5 Population
6 Income (dollars)
7 Median Age (years)
8 Single parent households (%)
9 Married households (%)
10 Households with children (%)
11 Average size of homehold (people)
12 College or Better (%)
